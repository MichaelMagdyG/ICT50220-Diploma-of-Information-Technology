About TasCollege

TasCollege, an Australian Registered Training Organization (RTO), has been operating under the Vocational Education and Training (VET) Quality Framework since 2018. We offer Australian Qualification Framework (AQF) Qualifications, sourced from Australian National Training Packages, which are not only recognized throughout Australia but also hold international acclaim.

TasCollege maintains campuses in Hobart (Head Office), Melbourne, and Sydney.
